Admission Information

University of Pan

The University of Pan offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs across different disciplines, including business, engineering, and social sciences.

  • Requirements:
    • High School Diploma or equivalent
    • Standardized Test Scores (e.g. SAT, ACT)
    • Application Essay
    • Letters of Recommendation
  • Admission Rate: 45%
  • School Fees: $10,000 per semester
